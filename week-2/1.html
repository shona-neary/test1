"the baltic sea- where amber is plenty and the sun doesn't set until late in the night"

It was early when a bus load of myself and 17 of my German relatives reached the border control from Poland to get into Russia. But why Russia? Well, it's not the typical "Russia" you may be thinking about- we were actually heading into Kaliningrad, a little section off of Russia which touches the Baltic Sea.
